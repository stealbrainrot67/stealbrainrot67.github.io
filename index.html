What Is Secret Brainrot 67?

The Secret Brainrot 67, introduced on September 13, 2025, is a coveted collectible within the game. This unique Brainrot stands out with its humanoid design, featuring white hands and shoes, and is characterized by the distinctive 6 and 7 particles it emits. Players can acquire Brainrot 67 by breaking open an Admin Lucky Block during special Admin Abuse events, where it has a rare drop rate of just 1%. With a staggering income potential of $7.5 million per second, it’s no wonder that players are eager to obtain this elusive item.
